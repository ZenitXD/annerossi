The founder

Anne Rossi

"Beauty Is Our Duty — and I have made it my life's work."

Anne Rossi is a registered nurse with a higher diploma in beauty therapy and aesthetic medicine. Throughout her nursing career, she always had a deep passion for skin and beauty — eventually stepping away from nursing to follow her true calling in the aesthetic world.

Anne opened her first clinic in Sutton, Dublin 13, before establishing her current home in Clontarf. Through extensive advanced training in London, Paris and Monaco, she has continually stayed at the forefront of innovation in aesthetic medicine — bringing world-class techniques to her Dublin clients.

Her nursing background isn't just a credential — it shapes every treatment she offers. Patient safety, clinical precision and genuine care are at the foundation of everything that happens at Anne Rossi Skin & Beauty.

Our space

The Clinic

A sanctuary in Clontarf

Located at 10c Kincora Avenue in the heart of Clontarf, our clinic is a calm, private space designed to make you feel completely at ease from the moment you arrive.

We combine a warm, welcoming environment with the clinical standards you would expect from a medically-led practice. Every room, every piece of equipment and every protocol reflects our commitment to both your comfort and your safety.

Convenient parking, accessible by public transport, and just minutes from Dublin city centre — we've made sure getting to us is as easy as possible.
