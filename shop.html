FAQs

Can I use the gift card for any treatment?
Yes — gift cards are valid for any treatment or service available at Anne Rossi Skin & Beauty, including all beauty and aesthetic medicine treatments.
Do gift cards expire?
Gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. We recommend booking an appointment soon after receiving one to avoid disappointment.
Can I buy more than €1,000?
The maximum per transaction is €1,000 (set by our payment processor). For a higher value, simply purchase two separate gift cards — both can be redeemed at the same appointment.
How is the gift card delivered?
Instantly by email. The recipient receives a digital gift card with a unique code they can present when booking or attending their appointment.
Can I get a refund on a gift card?
Gift cards are non-refundable but fully transferable — you can pass them on to someone else if needed. Please contact us if you have any concerns.
